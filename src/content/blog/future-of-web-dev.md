## The AI Revolution

Artificial Intelligence is no longer just a buzzword; it's a practical tool that is accelerating our workflows. Tools like GitHub Copilot and ChatGPT are not replacing developers but augmenting our capabilities.

### What This Means for Senior Devs

For senior developers, this shift requires a pivot in mindset. It's no longer just about writing syntax; it's about:

2.  **Prompt Engineering**: Learning to communicate effectively with LLMs.
3.  **Code Review**: shifting focus from syntax checking to logic and security verification.
